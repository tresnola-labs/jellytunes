# Privacy Policy

JellyTunes is developed by Orain Labs. We believe in transparency and minimal data collection.

## What We Collect

When you check for updates, JellyTunes sends a single anonymous request containing:
- **App version** (e.g., `v1.2.0`)
- **Platform** (e.g., `mac`, `windows`, `linux`)
- **Timestamp** of the request

No personal data is collected. No IP addresses are stored. No browsing behavior is tracked.

## Why

This anonymous data helps us understand which versions and platforms are in use, so we can prioritize bug fixes and compatibility improvements.

## How to Opt Out

Toggle **Anonymous usage statistics** off in the About screen. When disabled, no requests are sent to our analytics endpoint.
